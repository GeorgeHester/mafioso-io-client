Aim Of The Game Mafia:
If you are mafia your aim is to kill all of the villagers before they vote you out.

Other:
If you are any other role your aim is to protect the villagers and vote off the mafia.
Before Game Starts If you are the game host and enough players are in the game you can start the game by clicking the start game button at the bottom of the screen, you can invite more players by copying the game link at the bottom of the screen.
In Night Time Mafia:
If you are mafia you must decide who to kill by selecting the chosen player and clicking the confirm button at the bottom of the screen. Other mafias choices will show up as a counter on the players.

Doctor:
If you are the doctor you must select one person to immunise and confirm this by clicking the confirm button at the bottom of the screen.

Detective:
If you are the detective you must select one person to reveal the type of, you can confirm this selection by clicking the confirm button at the bottom of the screen.
In Day Time If you are still alive you have to select a player to vote out, once you have made your choice you must select the player and click the confirm button at the bottom of the screen.
After Game You will have the option to return to the main menu or if you are the host you will have the option to make a new game.
